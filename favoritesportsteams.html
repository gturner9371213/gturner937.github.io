Grillbillies is an animation inspired by famous series such as South Park, Squidbillies, and King of the Hill as well as the NFT craze in 2021. This video features a teaser in which an alien spaceship crashes, and an alien composed of a Mountain Dew bottle with Cheeto arms gives the Grillbillies a mission to save the metaverse from NFT scams. I voice a vulgar, chainsmoking degenerate named Cooter. I would recommend if you are into edgy comedy .
